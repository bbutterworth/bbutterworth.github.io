I am a geographer and atmospheric scientist. I study the physical processes affecting the exchange of carbon dioxide between the ocean and the atmosphere. Over the years I have had the opportunity to conduct field experiments in many exciting regions, including the Rocky Mountains, the Arctic, and Antarctic. I currently live in Calgary, Alberta, Canada. I just recently became a father to a daughter. I am excited to be a parent and to continue exploring with work and with my family.
